    General Services

    XFMS excels in diverse services, surpassing your expectations. Our offerings include Annual Maintenance Contracts, Landscaping, Carpentry & Painting, Building Renovation, Excavation, and Interior & Exterior Fitouts.

    MEP Services

    MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) forms the foundation for preserving a building's value, and FMS prioritizes expertise and continuous training in its technicians. Specializing in Air Conditioning, they handle various units and systems such as Fresh Air Handling Units, Fan Coil Units, Split A/C Units, Heat Exchangers, and Heat Recovery Wheels. Their comprehensive services encompass Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing systems, reflecting a commitment to maintaining and enhancing building functionality.

    HANDYMAN & FIT-OUTWORKS

    XFMS offers complete Interior Fit-Out Services, ensuring the polished completion of your property's interior. Our experts oversee a spectrum of commercial renovation requirements, encompassing both standard and bespoke electrical installations, carpet fitting, interior painting, furniture arrangement, and additional essential services.

    HVAC Services

    XFMS provides a variety of ventilation services for both commercial and residential properties, covering aspects such as kitchen extraction fans, stair pressurization systems, kitchen exhaust fans, and smoke extraction fans. Additionally, FMS offers comprehensive air conditioning services, including installation, maintenance, and emergency repair, conducted by highly skilled technicians.

    Landscaping and gardening works

    In urban environments marked by pollution and construction, FMS emphasizes the significance of landscaping and gardening for overall health and well-being. Their eco-friendly landscaping services involve the creation and upkeep of gardens, shrubs, and trees, ensuring they remain fresh and beautiful throughout the year.

    Indoor & Outdoor Cleaning Services

    FMS provides professional home cleaning services to address the challenge of maintaining a clean home amidst busy schedules. Their reliable cleaners offer both basic and deep cleaning, covering general cleaning, deep cleaning, and sanitization. The services extend to door and glass cleaning, as well as specialized cleaning for sofas, mattresses, carpets, balconies, and facades.
